Roadmap

            

                

                    
High Performance Computing

                    
I have worked in projects where high performance computing was a tier one priority. One such

Programming

                        worked
                        with Python, Java(Type)Script, C-like, Swift, Rust and Zig.

                

                

                    
Networking

                    
I have designed custom protocols over UDP, you can find an example of such in Nearssage's

Algorithm Research

                    
I have researched and worked on several algorithms to tackle some problems I stumbled upon. One
                        of those is

Cryptography

                    
For me cryptography is an essential in our daily lives, that's why I dedicated a tremendous
                        effort on researching the field. This effort created Komatta, a cryptosystem with a symetric

Blockchain

Compiler Design

                        generator is non existant).
